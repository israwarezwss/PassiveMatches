Why we built PassiveMatches

Most passive income content is generic. "Start a blog." "Buy index funds." "Open a Shopify store." None of it accounts for who you actually are — your available time, your budget, your skills, or your goals.

We built PassiveMatches to fix that. A 5-minute quiz that produces a genuinely personalized roadmap, scored against real earnings data from real platforms. No fluff. No upsells. Just your match.

🔬

How the matching works

Our algorithm scores 14 different income streams against your quiz answers — weighing time availability, starting budget, existing skills, risk tolerance, and financial goals. Each stream receives a fit score unique to your profile.

The result is a ranked list of streams most likely to work for someone exactly like you, with direct links to the platforms you need and clear guidance on how to start — today, not someday.

🤝

How we make money (and why it doesn't affect your results)

PassiveMatches is completely free to use. No account, no credit card, no hidden fees. We earn small affiliate commissions from partner platforms when you choose to sign up through a link on our results page. This comes at no cost to you — the platforms pay us a referral fee.

Crucially, affiliate relationships never influence our rankings. Every recommendation is driven entirely by how well an income stream fits your quiz profile. We'd rather give you the right answer than a profitable one.

📊

Our commitment to honest data

Every earnings figure shown on PassiveMatches is drawn from platform-published data, independent creator surveys, and publicly reported contributor statistics. They represent what active, consistent participants earn — not cherry-picked outliers or unrealistic promises.

We always display a range, never a guarantee. Your results depend on your effort, consistency, and circumstances — and we believe you deserve to know that upfront.
